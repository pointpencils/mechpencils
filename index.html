About Us

We have been making the highest quality mechanical pencils since 1952. Manufactured in the USA our pencils come in packs of 420. Each pencil extends to 3ft long, with 1ft made up of the actual pencils, while the other part of the pencil is made up of eraser. This system ensures your biggest mistakes will be erased. The technology used inside our pencils provide a professional pencil experience. Each time you click the top of the pencil to dispense lead, it will dispense just the right amount so you don’t get too little lead or too much lead. The plastic shell around our pencils is made from sardine oil. It provides a durable and soft gripping experience. Finally the lead used in our pencils is 0.5 lead, this lead has been proven to be the the perfect diameter for all types of writing. It is especially dark due to the mix of grounded bugs and graphite within the lead. The mix of ground bugs means that your pencil lead will be almost unbreakable.
